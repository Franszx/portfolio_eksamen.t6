Idea

BASIC ANIMATION

Starting point

  1. Name: Anders Lindberg
  2. Style starting point: Toca Band
  3. Gamename: Save the planet
  4. Game idea: Save the earth from evil Aliens who wants to overtake the earth and wipe out the population.

Style of the game and characters

I've come a long way since my first idea and I've been inspired on many websites when I've searched for aliens and space.
I started with the Kawaii style, but jumped towards toca band styles, but hit something that appealed to me by the style I found. I see it as a form of kawaii.

Name of the Game

The name of the game changed many times but ended with Space invaders.
I wanted a name that was more catchy and more suited to my idea and I think it sounds better and reminds me of a real game.
I know the real game Space Invaders, but I was not 100% inspired by the old game.
