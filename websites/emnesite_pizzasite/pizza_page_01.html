Pizza is a dish of Italian origin consisting(and really delicious), of a usually round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other meat or vegetable ingredients, which is then baked at a high temperature,traditionally in a wood-fired oven. YUM

Pizza should be considered a peace symbol among people and even nations. You find everything good on and in a pizza. Great tomatoes, awesome prosciutto and even exotic vegetable or the great pineapple.

Pizza often comes freshly baked or you will find a wide range of frozen pizzas which will come in handy when you want to save money or just be cool and go against the flow.

So for now enjoy the site and afterwords go and get pizza.

Fun fact history: The word pizza was first documented in 997 AD in Gaeta and successively in different parts of Central and Southern Italy. Pizza was mainly eaten in Italy and by emigrants from there. This changed after World War II when Allied troops stationed in Italy came to enjoy pizza along with other Italian foods.
